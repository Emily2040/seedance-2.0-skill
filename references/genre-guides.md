# Genre Guides

## Product Ad

Preserve product identity. Use one reveal, clean reflections, material-specific light, and controlled camera movement.

## Short Drama

Use gesture, blocking, silence, and a visible emotional turn. Keep the prompt anchored to one beat.

## Action

Use one objective, one obstacle, one camera move, and explicit character tags.

## Animation

Use medium, line quality, palette, deformation, timing, texture, and motion rhythm. Avoid named studio, franchise, or artist labels.

## Music / Beat Sync

Map beats to visible changes: step, cut, light pulse, object impact, or camera accent.
