# Quick Reference

## Default route

- Vague idea: `seedance-interview`.
- Clear idea: `seedance-prompt`.
- Short prompt: `seedance-prompt-short`.
- Bad result: `seedance-troubleshoot`.
- IP or real-person risk: `seedance-copyright`.
- Blocked prompt: `seedance-filter`.

## Prompt checklist

- Subject and action are explicit.
- Reference assets have roles.
- One primary camera move.
- Lighting source is physical.
- Audio intent is stated when relevant.
- Multi-character actions are assigned by tag.
- Protected identity is rewritten or authorized.
- Hollow quality words are removed.
